About the Author

Hello! I am the creator of this page, Lachlan Anderson. I was born and raised in Canada, and I'm currently a full-time student studying Computer Programming and Analysis at Seneca Polytechnic in Toronto, Ontario. I'm a cat-lover, passionate about proper animal care, an ethical vegetarian, and an avid fan of unique travel locations. I'm particularly interested in travelling to remote and rural destinations, where tourism is less common. I find the intersection between rural tourism and the ever looming economic decline in rural communities to be fascinating, as they create an indescribable internal emotional conflict. Visiting places that are almost certainly not going to exist even 5 or 10 years down the line is an incredibly thought provoking experience, which I cannot recommend more.

In my free time, when I'm not studying or attending classes, I spend quality time with my own two cats, Black Hole and Theia, both of whom are rescues. I'm also currently taking care of a pair of kittens, until they are able to go to their forever home.
